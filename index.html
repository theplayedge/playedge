WHAT IS THE PLAY EDGE?

At The Play Edge, we believe PLAY is how we learn best! So we design immersive, play-based experiences that nurture future-ready skills. Our sessions for children, training for teachers and products are not just games; they are carefully crafted learning journeys that make participants more curious, creative, and capable in an unpredictable world.

WHY WE EXIST?

The world, as we know it, is changing at an incredibly fast pace. And, technology is taking over our lives like never before. In this fast paced and uncertain world, some of the fundamental skills like resilience, growth mindset, curiosity, adaptability and problem solving will give our children the edge to thrive!

We believe we need to teach these skills more intentionally, using play as the medium.

OUR PROGRAMS

All our programs are designed to give agency to our participants and take ownership of their learning. We design our programs in experiential and playful formats, using a blend of free play, guided play, and custom games.

Each of our programs is designed to ultimately help children develop critical life-skills to thrive today and produce massive outcomes in the long term.

Know more Start playing today!

1. Future Leader Program

This in-depth program develops leadership skills, knowledge and mindsets for holistic development of children. Our monthly team challenges cover UNs sustainable development goals and end of project presentation showcases. We help children experience, understand and solve world challenges they face today.

2. STEAM Program

This hands-on program fosters a wide range of STEAM skills, like design, engineering, and scientific inquiry. We encourage real-world application of STEAM concepts through our bi-weekly projects. Through engaging challenges, participants work collaboratively to design, build, and innovate.

3. Workshops

These are 4-6 hr workshops on a single day. These workshops are meant to fun experiences for children to start their journey to learn new topics. These will be on different topics each time. Some examples could be Architecture, Design, Design thinking, Financial literacy etc.

OUR CORE PRINCIPLES OF PLAY

Free Play

Free play allows participants to explore, experiment, and create freely with peers without rigid rules. It fosters creativity, problem-solving, and intrinsic motivation by letting players take the lead in their learning journey.

Guided Play

Guided play blends freedom with structure, using prompts or challenges to steer learning while keeping it playful. Facilitators provide frameworks, guiding questions and challenges encouraging deeper thought and application through exploration and choice.

Custom Games

Custom-designed games align with specific learning objectives, making complex topics engaging and interactive. Through purposeful game mechanics, players develop targeted skills in an immersive and enjoyable way.
