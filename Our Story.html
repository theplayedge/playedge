THE PROBLEM

As we face a more uncertain world the skills taught in schools are not enough and in some cases will become irrelevant in the future.

51%

Graduates are unemployable. Core skills required are problem solving, communication and adaptability which are missing.

25%

Jobs will be affected in the form of new job creation or destruction of old jobs. This is from 2025-30. Most of the school going children will join workforce after 10 years.

23%

Children in school have a mental health issue in any given year. The number dramatically increases if incidents of extreme emotions are considered.

THE SOLUTION

We believe we need to intentionally teach skills the future needs. Going forward emotional intelligence, problem solving and creativity, entrepreneurial skills and mindset, and financial literacy will be key to thrive in career and for holistic wellbeing.

"Our aim is to harness the power of play to develop a 'Playful Attitude' in children to thrive today and create long term impact in the future."

Benefits of play as a medium of learning

Play is an intrinsic part of human development that transcends age boundaries. From childhood to adulthood, it serves as a dynamic tool for nurturing creativity, fostering emotional resilience and enhancing social connections. Play is not merely a leisure activity; it is a vital component of growth that supports individuals in becoming well-rounded and thriving members of society.

We believe developing these fundamental skills will allow children to:

  • Enhances cognitive skills such as critical thinking and problem-solving.
  • Builds social competencies like communication, empathy, and teamwork.
  • Provides emotional regulation through stress relief and self-expression.
  • Fosters creativity and innovation across all ages.
  • Strengthens interpersonal relationships.
  • Develops resilience by allowing experimentation in a safe context.
  • Provides better engagement and focus
  • Makes activities more hands on and active
